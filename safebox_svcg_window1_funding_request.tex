\section{Section B --- Goals for the Period}
Over this two-month Window 1 period, SafeBox Technologies will procure and clear \textbf{120 units of Mokosmart VT001-L trackers} and \textbf{120 matching H1 BLE keychain fobs} (deploying \textbf{100 units} into vehicle fleets and holding \textbf{20 units} on reserve for rapid hardware warranty swap-outs). We will onboard \textbf{5 corporate logistics fleets} (prospects include GIG Logistics, Kano Line Transport, Max.ng e-bikes, Lagos Haulage \& Distribution, and Amana Express), install trackers, register active connections on our telematics dashboard, and implement paid flows via Paystack. This commercial pilot will generate \textbf{\text{₦}7,000,000 in one-time hardware setup revenue} (100 deployed vehicles $\times$ \text{₦}70,000 one-time fee) and establish a monthly recurring subscription revenue target of \textbf{\text{₦}210,000} (reflecting a conservative 70\% active paying subscription rate of 70 paying subscriptions $\times$ \text{₦}3,000/mo). The pilot will validate our local Bluetooth-based Passive Keyless Entry (PKE) bypass, valet override switches, and fail-safe remote immobilization in real-world commercial fleet routes.
