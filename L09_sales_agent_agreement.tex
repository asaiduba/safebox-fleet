\section{Overview}
This evidence document contains the executed independent contractor sales agent agreements for the two (2) sales agents onboarded by SafeBox Technologies for the S-VCG Window 1 Commercial Pilot phase:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Turad Umar Hamza} (Sales Agent, Northern Region --- Kano Line / Amana Express Focus)
    \item \textbf{Aminu Uba Said} (Sales Agent, Southern Region --- Lagos Haulage / GIG Logistics Focus)
\end{enumerate}

Each agent is contracted under a monthly commission/retainer structure of \text{₦}110,000 per month for the 2-month pilot phase.

\noindent \textbf{This Agreement} is made and entered into this 16th day of June, 2026, by and between:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{SafeBox Technologies}, a transport security venture based at PEZ Carnegie Mellon University Africa, Kigali Innovation City (hereinafter referred to as the \textbf{"Company"}), and
    \item \textbf{Turad Umar Hamza}, residing in Kano, Nigeria (hereinafter referred to as the \textbf{"Representative"}).
\end{enumerate}

\section{Scope of Services}
The Representative is engaged as an independent contractor to represent the Company in the promotion, sales, and corporate onboarding of the SafeBox Fleet telematics and vehicle security platform. Responsibilities include:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Targeting and securing B2B corporate fleet accounts in Kano and northern transport corridors (e.g., Amana Express, Kano Line).
    \item Introducing logistics managers and fleet owners to the SafeBox telematics dashboard and remote engine immobilization technology.
    \item Acting as a primary contact for local B2B accounts during the commercial pilot phase.
\end{itemize}

\section{Compensation \& Terms}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Base Retainer / Draw:} The Representative shall receive a monthly base retainer of \text{₦}110,000 for the duration of the two-month commercial pilot phase (totaling \text{₦}220,000).
    \item \textbf{Duration:} This agreement shall be effective from June 16, 2026, and shall expire on August 15, 2026, unless extended by mutual written agreement.
\end{itemize}

\section{Performance Targets (KPIs)}
The Representative's performance will be measured against the following targets:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Onboarding at least two (2) corporate logistics fleets onto the SafeBox platform.
    \item Securing agreements for the installation of at least thirty (30) Mokosmart tracking units.
\end{itemize}
